The best source for quotes, of course, is the book itself. Every time I read it, some sentence or phrase stands out. For just quotes there are numerous spin-offs, like "The Tao of Pooh", "Winnie-The-Pooh's Little Book of Wisdom", and the "The Pooh Book of Quotations".
